###### Vancouver, Washington
*11-08-2019 - 11-11-2019*

We will be travelling to Vancouver, Washington for this year's Tennis on Campus Sectionals tournament!

**Date**

The tournament takes place from November 8th - 11th. We will need to leave Friday in the morning to play some matches in the evening.

The tournament will be at Tualatin Hills Tennis Center and Vancouver Tennis Center (5300 E 18th St, Vancouver, WA 98661).

**Leaving Times:**

We will leave in carpools on Friday to get to the tournament for matches, carpools will be emailed out before we leave.
